    First Amour

She never entered a room loudly.
She arrived softly, like evening light,
quiet enough to be missed by everyone except me.


Morning always felt slower near her,
sunlight resting quietly on her hair.
She never knew how often I looked away just to keep my heart from speaking.


There was something innocent about her.
The way she spoke gently,
the way she hid her thoughts behind small smiles,
the way she cared too much and never admitted it.


And maybe that is what first love really is.
Seeing someone ordinary to the world, but somehow feeling like they were made for your heart alone.


    -Aun Zafar
